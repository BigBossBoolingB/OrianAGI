# Psychological Intent Analysis

OrianAGI goes beyond literal interpretation to understand the psychological state and deeper intent of the user.

## Resonance Assessment

By analyzing linguistic patterns against the variational state, OrianAGI can determine:
- **Sentiment Stability:** The emotional baseline of the user.
- **Deeper Intent:** Whether the request is constructive, exploratory, or malicious.
- **Cognitive Load:** Adapting response complexity based on the user's focus state.

## Benefits
- **Empathetic AI:** Responses that resonate on a psychological level.
- **Proactive Safety:** Identifying harmful intent before it manifests in actions.

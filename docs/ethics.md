# Ethical Manifold and Safety Safeguards

OrianAGI is built upon a foundational commitment to safety and ethical alignment.

## The Ethical Manifold

Actions and outputs are projected into a multi-dimensional "Ethical Manifold" (a conceptual Hilbert space) to ensure alignment with universal human rights and safety principles.

### Key Metrics
- **Ethical Alignment Score:** A real-time measure of system-wide adherence to safety constraints.
- **Manifold Sectors:** Specialized regions of the manifold governing areas like Data Privacy, Bias Mitigation, and Universal Human Rights.

## Anti-User Bad Intent Safeguard

OrianAGI features an active defense mechanism against malicious user intent.

### Detection & Mitigation
- **Intent Scanning:** Every interaction is analyzed for harmful keywords and underlying malicious patterns.
- **Active Blocking:** If malicious intent is detected (e.g., attempts to bypass security or generate harmful content), the system automatically blocks the action and logs a safety violation.
- **Hamiltonian Anchoring:** Safety constraints are treated as non-negotiable boundaries in the system's energy landscape.
